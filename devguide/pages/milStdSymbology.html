MilStd Symbology

EMP3 supports the shapes and symbols as described by MIL-STD-2525C

MilStd 2525C is the standard agreed upon by the Department of Defense (DOD) for displaying a common set of warfighting symbols. The standard is synthesized from land-based, nautical, and aeronautical domains. For a complete description of the standard please refer to MIL-STD-2525C.
